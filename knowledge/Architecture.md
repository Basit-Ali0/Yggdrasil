## ğŸ”’ Security & Auth Wrapper

**MVP Auth Strategy:**
- Toggle: `NEXT_PUBLIC_DEMO_MODE=true`
- Logic: `if (DEMO_MODE) useHardcodedSession() else useSupabaseAuth()`
